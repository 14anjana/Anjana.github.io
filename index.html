Research and Publication

Analyzing The Relationship Between Music Signals And Brain Signals Using Machine Learning

Anjana P Yadahalli, Nidhishree V M, Swaraj M H

Second International Conference on Advances in Smart Sensor, Signal Processing and Communication Technology (ICASSCT 2024) March 22 - 23, Goa University, India

The study explores how different musical stimuli affect the brain using Emotiv 14 channel EEG device to capture responses. Features extracted via Fast Fourier Transform were used to train machine learning models like Logistic Regression, SVM, and Random Forest to categorize mental states induced by music. Results showed significant EEG response variations across age groups, offering insights into personalized music therapy and mental health management.
